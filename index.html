Tiny Leaders Reignited

Tiny leaders Reignited is a web page dedicated to keeping current banlist updates and format asjustments for the Tiny Leaders format which has been without guidance for many years.

What is Tiny Leaders?

To quote the original Tiny Leaders blog post: "The rules are simple- 50 card decks, including your "Commander", with no more than one of each card (a "Highlander" format), and no card shall exceed converted mana cost 3."


Additionally, Tiny Leaders decks have access to a 10 card sideboard.

What are the rules?

While the scentence above gives a good summary of the rules, a more detailed list can be found below. For any rules questions not covered by the below list, please refer to the Commander rules.

  1. Tiny Leaders is a 1v1, best-of-three format
  2. Players begin the game with 25 life
  3. Decks are comprised of 50 cards including the commander
  4. No more than one of each card
  5. No card can cost more than 3 mana
  6. Your commander can be a legendary creature (or 2 in the case of cards with the partner ability) or a cmc 3 or less planeswalker.
  7. Each card in a deck must not have mana symbols not found in their commander's color identity
  8. Each deck can have a sideboard of up to 10 cards. Commanders can not be changed between games.
  9. Commanders start in the Command Zone and are cast under normal timing restrictions. Owners must pay 2 generic mana more to cast their commander for each time that commander has been cast from the Command Zone that game.
  10. If a commander is put into your graveyard or the exile zone, you may chose to leave it there or move it to the Command Zone.
